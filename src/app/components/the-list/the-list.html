Expression of Cooperative
Cultural Heritage

An expression of Cooperative Cultural Heritage is a distinctive practice, place, tradition,
institution, or system that embodies the values, identity, and legacy of cooperation.

Tangible:

Buildings, settlements, archives, landscapes, ...

Intangible:

Practices, traditions, knowledge, skills, expressions, ...

Each heritage element is recognized because:

1.

Embodies cooperative principles (democracy, fairness, solidarity, mutual support).

2.

Holds cultural, social, or historical significance for a community or region.

3.

Conveys cooperative identity across generations.

4.

Demonstrates continuity and innovation, connecting past traditions with present and future needs.

How will the cultural
expressions be selected?

The selection will follow clear and transparent standards to reflect the
diversity and richness of the global cooperative tradition.
